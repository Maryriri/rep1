Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
  • 1 lb spaghetti

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  2. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, grated carrot, and chopped celery to the pot. Saut√© until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and red wine (if using). Stir in tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce over low heat for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with grated Parmesan cheese on the side.
  8. Enjoy your hearty and delicious Spaghetti Bolognese!
