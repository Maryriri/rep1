Grilled Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Thread chicken, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers.
  3. Brush skewers with the prepared marinade.
  4. Grill for 10-15 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
