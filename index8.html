Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Season chicken strips with salt and pepper, then cook until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream, bring to a simmer, and then reduce the heat to low.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce thickens.
  6. Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet, stirring to coat the chicken in the Alfredo sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  7. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the Chicken Alfredo over the cooked fettuccine pasta.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and additional Parmesan if desired. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Alfredo Pasta!
