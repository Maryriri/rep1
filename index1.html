Simple Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the pasta mixture and toss until well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy your delicious pasta salad!
