Classic Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 anchovy fillets, minced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and croutons.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, minced anchovies (if using), Worcestershire sauce, and red wine vinegar.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while continuously whisking to emulsify the dressing.
  4. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Pour the dressing over the romaine lettuce and croutons. Toss gently to coat the salad evenly.
  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the salad.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your classic Caesar Salad!
