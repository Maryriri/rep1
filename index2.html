Refreshing Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 2 cups pineapple, diced
  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup kiwi, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and prepare all the fruits as needed.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the watermelon, pineapple, strawberries, grapes, blueberries, and kiwi.
  3. If desired, drizzle honey over the fruits for added sweetness. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Chill the fruit salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Before serving, give the fruit salad a gentle toss to make sure the flavors are well combined.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a burst of color and extra freshness.
  7. Serve the refreshing fruit salad in bowls or on a platter. Enjoy!
